## 4) Visual & Interaction Spec (Plain Language)

### Layout & Spacing
- Generous white space  
- Clear vertical rhythm  
- Section separation via spacing, not dividers  

### Typography Feel
- Editorial and highly readable  
- Strong hierarchy: headline → subhead → body → meta  
- Calm, intellectual tone  

### Color Palette
- Soft neutral background (off-white or muted gray)  
- Dark, high-contrast text  
- One restrained accent color for links and CTA  

### Buttons & Links
- Minimal styling  
- No decorative effects  
- Emphasis through placement, not color intensity  

### Motion Principles
- Subtle fade-in on scroll  
- No parallax, no looping animations  
- Motion supports calmness, not attention-seeking  

### Mobile Behavior
- Single-column layout  
- Text-first priority  
- CTA and contact form easily reachable without being sticky  

---

## 5) Acceptance Checklist (Testable)

- Feels senior and credible within the first few seconds  
- Investor understands who this is without scrolling far  
- No hype, no marketing language  
- CTA is clear but selective  
- Contact form is minimal and functional  
- Smooth scrolling, no visual noise  
- Fully readable and elegant on mobile  
- No content beyond CV-verified facts  
- No placeholders visible at launch
